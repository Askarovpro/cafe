# Kamolliddin — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Cafe/restaurant staff in Uzbekistan (Uzbek UI, UZS). Four roles, four surfaces:
- **Manager** (Telegram mini app) — B2B clients, per-client pricing, orders, delivery dispatch, ledger.
- **Kitchen** (wall-monitor web KDS) — 3-column order line, glanceable from across the kitchen.
- **Driver** (Telegram mini app) — assigned deliveries, navigation, cash held, hand-off.
- **Moliyachi / Finance** (Telegram mini app) — cash reconciliation from drivers/managers,
  treasury (cash in/out), salaries & advances.
Used on phones during a busy shift; the KDS on a mounted monitor. Speed and clarity beat decoration.

### Brand Personality
Operational, trustworthy, quick. Three words: **utilitarian, warm, precise.** It should feel like a
well-run kitchen pass — everything legible at a glance, money always accounted for, no ceremony.

### Aesthetic Direction
Clean iOS operations tool (reference: the "Move." delivery app). White cards on a warm light-grey
page, soft shadows, generous radius, a golden saffron accent (plov saffron, not the AI cream+terracotta
default), near-black pill headers/CTAs. The signature is the **order docket** (kitchen-ticket) with a
monospace number. KDS is the deliberate exception — its own dark, high-contrast monitor identity.
Anti-references: rainbow status chips, glassmorphism, gradient text, generic dashboard hero-metric tiles.

### Design Principles
1. **Money is legible.** Amounts in tabular monospace; who holds cash is always visible; states are named.
2. **Restrained color.** Gold = action/in-progress, green = done, red = cancel, neutral = waiting. Color is rare and meaningful; the KDS keeps functional column colors.
3. **One system across surfaces.** Shared tokens + docket + icons (HugeIcons, no emoji). A manager and a driver clearly use the same product.
4. **Glanceable hierarchy.** Big number + small label for the one thing that matters on a screen; everything else quiet.
5. **Native, not templated.** Telegram theme-aware mini apps; bottom-tab navigation; real empty states that teach.
